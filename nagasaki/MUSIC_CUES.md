## Silence Map

- Scene 01 opens on ambient-only air for the first beat before the low tone arrives.
- Scene 06 should leave audible space after the funeral-return prayer; do not score over every word.
- Scene 11 requires 1.5 seconds of total silence immediately before the 11:02 impact line.
- Scene 13 should nearly stop breathing before the rosary macro, then re-enter with the faintest sacred overtone.
- Scene 15 ends in a slow decay rather than a triumphant cadence; let the bell resonance finish the film.
