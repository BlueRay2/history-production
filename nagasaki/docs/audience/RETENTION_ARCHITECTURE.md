## Pattern interrupt schedule

Each ~30–60 s transition must land one of:
- Visual scale shift (aerial → face; harbor → cathedral interior)
- Audio register shift (ambient sea → church bell → air raid siren)
- Era jump via J-cut (word ends in one era, voice of next era begins)
- Character switch (tsūji → peasant → doctor)
- Fact-density burst (statistic stack) → then slow personal moment

## Audio-physiological anchor points

- **Shepard tone** (ascending) during Fat Man descent (scene 11)
- **Silence (~1.5 s)** before 11:02 detonation (scene 11)
- **Erhu or shakuhachi** in Urakami village scenes (06), ink-wash in Dejima scenes (03)
- **Emotional contagion via narrator voice** in scene 13 (bones in ashes)
- **Resolution cadence** in scene 15 — lowest register, slowest pace, measured pauses

## Forbidden moves

- No pre-resolution of any loop before its scheduled scene
- No mushroom cloud as first image
- No rapid MTV-cuts during emotional beats (scenes 07, 13)
- No narrative device-reveal ("now I'm going to tell you about…") — let each era land via J-cut
