## 6. Budget Optimization Notes

1. **63% of clips are FREE** (Flow Fast) — excellent Premium Start optimization
2. **Sora 2 Relaxed mode** saves ~$47.50 per video (wait 3-30 min per clip vs instant)
3. **Kling usage is minimal** (0.9% of monthly credits) — could shift complex camera shots to Kling if Flow results are unsatisfactory, at negligible cost
4. **ElevenLabs well within Creator plan** — no upgrade needed unless producing 5+ videos/month
5. **At 4 videos/month**, amortized subscription cost drops to **$163/video**
6. **Most expensive line item:** Sora 2 subscription ($200/mo) for only 5 clips. Consider:
   - Using Relaxed mode exclusively (eliminates marginal cost)
   - Replacing 2-3 Sora clips with Kling Professional (better camera control, $0.49/clip vs $9.50/clip)
   - Fisherman character in medium/wide instead of close-up to avoid face rendering requirements
